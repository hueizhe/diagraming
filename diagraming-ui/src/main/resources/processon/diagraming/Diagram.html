Edit a Shape

To draw connectors between shapes, click on any point on the shape's edge. Hold down the mousebutton and drag the line into the desired direction. (The small circles are only suggestions.)

Change the shape's size using the small rectangles. (Press CTRL to maintain the shape's proportions.)
